Once a mudbrick waystation along desert trading routes, Riyadh is now one of the wealthiest cities in the world.

Riyadh (/rɨˈjɑːd/; Arabic: الرياض‎ ar-Riyāḍ pronounced [arːiˈjɑːdˤ],"The Gardens") is the capital and largest city of Saudi Arabia.

The city is divided into 15 municipal districts, managed by Riyadh Municipality headed by the mayor of Riyadh, and the Riyadh Development Authority, chaired by the governor of Riyadh Province, Khalid bin Bandar Al Saud.
